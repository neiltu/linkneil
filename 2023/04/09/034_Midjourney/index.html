請幫我構思一個故事的情節，主角是一個叫做麗莎的女學生，他對任何事物都充滿好奇心。再幫我加上一些時空的元素，以及在穿越時空之後跟各種名人對話
請給我一些ai繪圖的提示(prompts)，提示的內容是麗莎跟自己位有名的人交談，並詳細敘述每個場地的環境例如室內的擺飾和氣氛（描述場地是不用提到人名）
• Lisa meets with Albert Einstein in a laboratory, filled with equipment and scientific instruments.
• Lisa finds herself in a luxurious palace, talking with Cleopatra. The surroundings are opulent and decorated with gold and precious jewels.
• Lisa travels back to ancient China and has a conversation with Confucius in a tranquil garden. There are bamboo trees, a koi pond, and a small bridge over a stream.
• Lisa goes to a futuristic city and meets with Elon Musk in a sleek and modern office. The office is filled with high-tech gadgets and has a breathtaking view of the skyline.
• Lisa visits a temple and meets with an ancient Zen master. The temple is filled with ancient silk paintings and Buddha statues, and the candles cast a warm glow over the room.
