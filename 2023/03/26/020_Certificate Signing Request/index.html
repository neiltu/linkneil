Certificate Signing Request(CSR)

Certificate Signing Request(CSR)

In terms of certificate usage, the roles played by CSR (Certificate Signing Request) and CER (Certificate) are as follows:

  • CSR is a file containing encrypted keys and certificate-related information generated by the applicant, which is used by the applicant to request a certificate from a certificate authority (CA).
  • CER is a certificate file signed by a CA that contains the applicant’s public key and other related information, which is used to identify the applicant on the Internet.
    在憑證使用上，CSR (Certificate Signing Request) 和 CER (Certificate) 扮演的角色如下：
  • CSR 是由申請者產生的加密金鑰和證書相關資訊的檔案，申請者使用它來向證書授權機構 (CA) 申請證書。
  • CER 是由 CA 簽署的包含申請者公開金鑰和其他相關資訊的證書檔案，它用於在網際網路上識別申請者。

Certificate(CSR)

In short, CSR is the request generated by the user to obtain a certificate, while CER is the certificate itself signed by the CA.
Usually, it is said that CER is provided to the other company to achieve transaction purposes. After receiving the CER, the other party needs to do the following:

  • Verify the authenticity of the CER: The other party needs to confirm that the CER is signed by a legitimate CA to ensure that the certificate is genuine and trustworthy.
  • Install the CER: The other party needs to install the CER on its server to enable secure website communication or other secure communications.
  • Update the CER: CERs usually have a certain validity period, and the other party needs to update the CER before it expires to ensure the security and reliability of the website.
    簡單來說，CSR 是用戶生成證書的請求，而 CER 是由 CA 簽署的證書本身。
    通常會講 CER 提供給對方公司達成交易目的，對方收到 CER 之後需要做以下幾件事情：
  • 驗證 CER 的真實性：對方需要確認 CER 是由合法的 CA 簽署的，以確保該證書是真實可信的。
  • 安裝 CER：對方需要將 CER 安裝在其服務器上，以便進行網站加密通信或進行其他安全通訊。
  • 更新 CER：CER 通常有一定的有效期限，對方需要在到期之前更新 CER，以確保網站的安全性和可靠性。
